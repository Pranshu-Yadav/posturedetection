Diet Chart for Different Age Groups

Age Group Recommended Diet
Children (6-12 years) Balanced diet with emphasis on calcium, protein, and vitamins. Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and dairy products.
Teens (13-19 years) High in protein and iron. Include lean meats, eggs, nuts, legumes, fruits, and vegetables. Ensure adequate calcium intake.
Adults (20-50 years) Balanced diet with a focus on lean proteins, whole grains, and plenty of fruits and vegetables. Limit processed foods and sugar.
Seniors (50+ years) High fiber and low-fat diet. Include whole grains, lean proteins, fruits, and vegetables. Ensure adequate vitamin D and calcium.
